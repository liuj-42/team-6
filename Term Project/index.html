Welcome to the Mental Health for Students website.

We know how hard and frustrating it can be to be a college student, especially now with a lot of classes being online and a lot of facilities not being open. College can be a stressful experience for many students and it can have numerous damaging effects on their mental health and well-being.

This is why this site exists, to provide you with all the resources that can help you with stress, anxiety, frustration, being overwhelmed, and other challenges faced by college students daily.


From this page, you can travel to three main pages:

Stress Alleviation Categories
This is a hub for different types of dealing with stress and better managing your workload in college.

Mental Health Form
A survey where you can share how you're doing overall, the amount of stress you feel from your classes, general questions about the amount of sleep you're getting, your diet, how your time management is like, etc., and other comments relating to mental health.

Other Helpful Resources
A page that contains links to other helpful resources for mental health to further help you.



Some common tips for dealing with stress:

  • Manage your time so that you have enough time for work from classes and time for yourself
  • Take breaks and do what you like doing the most from time to time (hobbies)
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from others
  • Breathing exercises
  • Stay healthy & active: eat healthy foods, get a good amount of sleep, and exercise your body
  • Encourage yourself to have a positive mindset
